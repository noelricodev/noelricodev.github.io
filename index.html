IDEM (Infectious Disease Exposure Map)

When the COVID-19 reaches the Philippines, my previous team decided to develop a contact tracing app. The objectives of this app are to make the contact tracing more easier and avoid physical contact when using a paper and a pen from the establishments that can possibly cause a virus transmission. The users will just declare all of the signs and symptoms they are currently experiencing then the app will rate the user based on the signs and symptoms they have selected, the ratings are also based on DOH data. The app also have a QR code, this will scan the unique QR code provided to the establishments they are visiting. The app was made using Laravel Framework for the API and Ionic Framework with Angular for the mobile app.
